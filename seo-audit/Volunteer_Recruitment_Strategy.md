# Volunteer Recruitment Strategy
## Businesses Beyond Borders - Volusia County Focus

---

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive volunteer recruitment strategy targets Volusia County, Florida residents while leveraging your organization's unique international mission. By combining local community engagement with global impact messaging, we can significantly increase volunteer applications and long-term commitment.

---

## Current Volunteer Landscape Analysis

### **Volusia County Volunteer Environment**
- **United Way of Volusia-Flagler Counties** coordinates 100+ nonprofit organizations
- **Active volunteer base** includes retirees, college students, and working professionals
- **High demand** for meaningful, skills-based volunteer opportunities
- **Growing interest** in international development and remote volunteering

### **Key Demographics to Target**

#### **Primary Target: Retirees (Ages 55-75)**
- **Location:** Port Orange, Ormond Beach, Daytona Beach Shores
- **Motivation:** Desire for meaningful impact, time availability
- **Skills:** Business experience, financial knowledge, mentorship abilities
- **Approach:** Emphasize global impact from local base

#### **Secondary Target: College Students (Ages 18-25)**
- **Institutions:** Stetson University (DeLand), UCF Daytona Beach, Daytona State College
- **Motivation:** Resume building, international experience, social impact
- **Skills:** Technology, social media, language skills
- **Approach:** Career development + global experience

#### **Tertiary Target: Working Professionals (Ages 30-55)**
- **Industries:** Healthcare, finance, education, technology
- **Motivation:** Skills-based volunteering, networking, corporate social responsibility
- **Skills:** Professional expertise, project management
- **Approach:** Flexible, skills-based opportunities

## Volunteer Onboarding Process

### **Phase 1: Interest Generation (Week 1)**
1. **Initial Contact** - Volunteer inquiry form submission
2. **Welcome Package** - Organization overview, impact stories
3. **Informal Coffee Chat** - 30-minute virtual or in-person meeting

### **Phase 2: Skill Assessment (Week 2)**
1. **Skills Survey** - Detailed assessment of volunteer capabilities
2. **Interest Matching** - Program area alignment discussion
3. **Time Commitment** - Realistic expectation setting

### **Phase 3: Training & Integration (Week 3-4)**
1. **Organization Training** - Mission, values, programs overview
2. **Specific Training** - Role-specific skill development
3. **Mentor Assignment** - Pairing with experienced volunteer

### **Phase 4: Active Engagement (Ongoing)**
1. **Regular Check-ins** - Monthly volunteer satisfaction surveys
2. **Recognition Program** - Quarterly volunteer appreciation events
3. **Growth Opportunities** - Leadership development pathways

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### **Common Volunteer Challenges**

#### **Time Commitment Over-Promise**
- **Solution:** Clear expectation setting during onboarding
- **Prevention:** Flexible scheduling options

#### **Skills Mismatch**
- **Solution:** Detailed skills assessment process
- **Prevention:** Multiple volunteer pathway options

#### **Communication Barriers**
- **Solution:** Regular check-ins and feedback loops
- **Prevention:** Mentor assignment system

#### **Volunteer Burnout**
- **Solution:** Recognition program and rotation options
- **Prevention:** Realistic workload distribution
